FAQ

1. What does it mean that my project is Encrypted?
As you could probably see, we attach key in your snippets URL. It is generated whenever you click 'Share', and whatever you have pasted and shared using CODYPASTE, gets encrypted. It means that we do not collect your snippets source code, we only store its encrypted version which could be readable only when mentioned key is provided by users.
2. How do we remember your shared projects?
Shared projects are stored in local storage (read more about it here). We do not store any private data (such as your snippets content), only id and encryption key, which are used to construct URL for your data, whenever you chose any of previously shared projects from list. You can easily remove stored data from local storage, for more details follow this article.
3. What data CODYPASTE.io collects and why?
We collect and store webserver logs like most websites on the internet and things that you have uploaded using our service in encrypted form.
